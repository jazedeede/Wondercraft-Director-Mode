What is Director Mode?

Director Mode lets you turn AI voices into hyper-realistic, natural speech , just by typing. Welcome to studio-grade audio!

When Should I Use it?

Director Mode works well for both general prompts (e.g. 'say this in a French accent') and specific additions (e.g. 'add a small pause after each sentence'). Use Director Mode to perfect your tracks with pauses, interjections and filler words - or just let your imagination run wild!

Step-By-Step Guide

  1. Create a track. You can create a blank track by clicking on ‘My Projects’ in the sidebar, or you can use a Wondercraft template, found on the homepage.
  2. Create a script . You can type a script, or you can let Wondercraft generate a script by typing a prompt or browsing our prompt library.
  3. Choose a voice to deliver your script. You can filter our voice library by gender, characteristics, accent and more. When you’re ready, apply it to either the entire script, or a single segment.
  4. Choose the segment you’d like to alter, and click on ‘Director Mode’.
  5. Type and send your prompt . Director Mode will generate a preview, which you can listen to by clicking play. Continue providing prompts until you find what you are looking for. When you’re happy with the audio, click ‘Approve take’.

Please note that generating previews in Director Mode consumes 4 credits/minute.

  1. Once approved, the new audio will appear in your track timeline. You can continue editing by adding content, music and sound effects.

Tips and Tricks

  • For best results, use Director Mode on segments of 30 words or less.
  • You can add multiple prompts separately, to the same voice segment. Director Mode will remember each prompt, and combine them until a take is approved.
  • If you’d like even more control over a segment’s intonation, try using 'Parrot Mode' !
